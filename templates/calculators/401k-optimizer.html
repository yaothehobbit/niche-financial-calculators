Don't Leave Free Money on the Table

An employer 401(k) match is essentially a guaranteed, immediate 50-100% return on your investment. No other investment comes close. If your employer matches 100% up to 6% of your salary, contributing less than 6% means you're literally turning down free money.

2026 Contribution Limits

  • Under 50: $23,500 employee limit
  • Ages 50-59 and 64+: $31,000 (with $7,500 catch-up)
  • Ages 60-63: $34,750 (with $11,250 super catch-up)
  • Total (employee + employer): $70,000

Common Match Formulas

  • 100% of first 6%: Most generous common match. Contribute 6% to get full match.
  • 50% of first 6%: Very common. Still contribute 6% to maximize — you get 3% free.
  • 100% of first 3%, 50% of next 2%: Tiered match. Contribute 5% to maximize — you get 4% free.
  • Dollar-for-dollar up to 3%: Simple but less generous. Contribute 3% minimum.
